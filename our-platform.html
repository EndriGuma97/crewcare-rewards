CrewCare Credit-Pal

Today's economy runs on credit. So if you want a mortgage for a house or a student loan to pay for college—or if you wish to charge your lunch on a credit card—you'll need a lender to extend you a line of credit.

People also need to be worthy of that line of credit. A three-digit credit score defines creditworthiness and is the key to financial livelihood. Good credit can be the make-or-break detail determining whether people get a mortgage, car loan, or student loan. On the other hand, bad credit will make it difficult to get a credit card with a low-interest rate and make it more expensive to borrow money for any purpose.

Our Credit-Pal platform is designed to help your employees understand their current credit situation and then take steps to improve their scores through several proven strategies.
